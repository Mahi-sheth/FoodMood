Cheese Toast

Crunchy bread topped with melted cheese, perfect for a quick snack.

Ingredients:

  • Bread slices
  • Grated cheese
  • Butter
  • Chili flakes, oregano

Steps:

  1. Spread butter on bread slices.
  2. Top with grated cheese and seasoning.
  3. Toast in a pan or oven until cheese melts.
