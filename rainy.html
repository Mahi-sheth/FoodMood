Masala Chai

A comforting blend of tea, milk, spices, and sweetness‚Äîperfect for cozy rainy days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • ¬Ω cup milk
  • 1 tsp tea leaves
  • 2 crushed cardamom pods
  • 1 small piece ginger (grated)
  • 1-2 tsp sugar (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Boil water with ginger and cardamom.
  2. Add tea leaves and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Add milk and sugar, then bring to a boil.

Tomato Soup

A classic comfort soup made with ripe tomatoes, herbs, and a dash of cream.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup vegetable broth or water
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Saut√© onion and garlic in a pan.

  5. Serve hot with croutons or bread.

Hot Chocolate

Creamy, dreamy hot cocoa made with real chocolate‚Äîpure winter bliss in a mug!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 50g dark chocolate (chopped)
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan.
  2. Add cocoa powder, sugar, and chocolate.
  3. Stir until smooth and melted.
  4. Pour into mug, top with cream, and enjoy!
